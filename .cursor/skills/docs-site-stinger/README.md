# docs-site-stinger

The `docs-site-stinger` equips `docs-site-worker-bee` to select, set up, and maintain developer-facing documentation sites. It covers the full 2026 platform landscape — Docusaurus v3/v4, Mintlify, GitBook, MkDocs Material (maintenance mode), Nextra v4, Starlight (Astro), and Fern — plus the Diátaxis content architecture model, docs-as-code CI pipelines, and search setup.

**Critical 2026 context:** MkDocs Material entered maintenance mode in November 2025. Starlight (Astro) is the recommended greenfield choice as of 2026.

This stinger was forged from `ai-tools/command-briefs/docs-site-worker-bee-command-brief.md` and research gathered in `research/research-summary.md` (depth: normal, window: Nov 2025 - May 2026, 14 source files).
