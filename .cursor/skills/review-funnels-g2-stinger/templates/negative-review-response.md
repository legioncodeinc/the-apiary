# Negative Review Response Templates

Fill-in-the-blank response templates by star-rating band. Always follow the Acknowledge / Clarify / Resolve / Close framework from `guides/05-negative-review-response.md`.

---

## 1-Star Response

**Use when:** Customer experienced a significant failure (data loss, broken core feature, billing error, support non-response).

---

Hi [Name / "there" if anonymous],

Thank you for sharing this feedback. [Specific issue mentioned in review] is a serious matter, and I don't want to minimize that.

[OPTIONAL: One-sentence clarification if there is a factual error. Otherwise, delete this line.]

I've already [specific action: e.g., "escalated this to our engineering team" / "reviewed your account" / "contacted our billing team on your behalf"]. I'd like to resolve this directly — please reach out to me at [email/support channel] and I'll personally make sure this is addressed.

I appreciate you taking the time to share your experience. This feedback helps us do better.

[Your name]
[Title], [Company]

---

## 2-Star Response

**Use when:** Customer experienced significant issues but there are also positive elements in the review, or the complaint is specific and addressable.

---

Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to leave this feedback. I hear you on [specific issue] — that's not the experience we want for our customers.

[OPTIONAL: If there's a factual correction to make, state it once, politely. Otherwise delete.] 

[What we've done or are doing: e.g., "We've made improvements to [area] since your experience" / "This is on our roadmap for Q[X]" / "I'd like to connect you with our team to work through this."]

If you'd like to discuss directly, I'm at [email]. We value your business and want to earn a better experience for you.

[Your name]

---

## 3-Star Response

**Use when:** Mixed review -- some positives, one or two specific criticisms. Often the most actionable type.

---

Hi [Name],

Thank you for the honest feedback and for highlighting what's working.

You're right that [specific criticism] hasn't been where it needs to be. [What's being done: e.g., "We've heard similar feedback and have [improvement] launching in [timeframe]" / "I'd love to learn more about your specific situation so we can address it."]

We'd love to earn a higher rating as [specific improvement] matures. Feel free to reach out at [email] if there's anything we can help with in the meantime.

[Your name]

---

## 4-Star Response

**Use when:** Mostly positive review with one specific gap mentioned.

---

Hi [Name],

Thank you for the kind words and for the specific feedback on [gap mentioned].

[One sentence on what you're doing to address the gap.]

We're glad [Product] has been valuable for [use case mentioned], and we look forward to keeping that track record going. Don't hesitate to reach out at [email] if anything else comes up.

[Your name]

---

## Notes

- Keep all responses under 150 words (shorter is more professional on review platforms).
- Never offer discounts or gifts in a public response.
- Never ask a reviewer to change their rating in the public response.
- For responses to suspected fraudulent reviews, consult `guides/05-negative-review-response.md` (decision tree section) before responding.
