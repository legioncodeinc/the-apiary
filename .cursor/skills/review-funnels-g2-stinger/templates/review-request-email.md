# Review Request Email Templates

Three variants for different trigger moments. Replace all `[PLACEHOLDER]` values before sending.

---

## Variant A: Activation milestone trigger (no incentive)

**Trigger:** User completes [specific milestone -- e.g., "first export", "third project", "100 tasks completed"]
**From:** Founder or CSM (personal name, not company alias)
**Timing:** Within 24 hours of milestone event

---

**Subject:** Quick favor?

Hi [FirstName],

I noticed you just [completed X milestone in Product] — congrats on hitting that milestone.

We're a small team, and reviews on G2 from customers like you make a real difference in helping other [ICP description, e.g., "project managers at growing startups"] find us.

Would you mind leaving a quick review? It takes about 2 minutes.

[Leave a G2 review →]  
https://www.g2.com/products/[YOUR-PRODUCT-SLUG]/reviews/new

Thanks for being a [Product] customer.

[Your name]
[Title], [Company]

---

## Variant B: NPS promoter follow-up (with incentive)

**Trigger:** NPS score of 9 or 10 received within past 7 days
**From:** Founder or CSM
**Timing:** Within 48 hours of NPS response
**Incentive:** $10-$25 gift card (verify G2 compliance per `guides/02-g2-incentive-policy.md` before sending)

---

**Subject:** Your [Product] experience -- and a small thank-you

Hi [FirstName],

Thanks for the kind words in your recent survey — scores like yours genuinely make our week.

We'd love to share your experience more broadly. Would you consider posting a quick review on G2? It takes about 2-3 minutes.

As a thank-you, I'll send you a [$X gift card platform] after you post — offered regardless of your rating. G2 guidelines ask that you mention receiving this incentive in your review.

[Leave a G2 review →]  
https://www.g2.com/products/[YOUR-PRODUCT-SLUG]/reviews/new

Once I see your review live, I'll send the thank-you your way.

Thanks,
[Your name]

P.S. If you have any feedback you'd prefer to share privately instead, I'm always at [email].

---

## Variant C: Annual renewal touchpoint

**Trigger:** 30 days after annual renewal confirmed
**From:** Account manager or CSM
**Timing:** 30 days post-renewal
**Incentive:** Optional (stronger without incentive for high-touch accounts)

---

**Subject:** [Company name] → one year on [Product]

Hi [FirstName],

It's been a year since [Company name] joined [Product] — and we're glad you renewed.

We'd love to capture your experience at this milestone. Would you be willing to share a brief review on G2? Reviews from customers who've used the product for a full year carry significant weight for buyers evaluating us.

[Share a review on G2 →]  
https://www.g2.com/products/[YOUR-PRODUCT-SLUG]/reviews/new

If there's anything on your mind — positive or constructive — feel free to share it with me directly too.

Thanks for trusting us with [their use case].

[Your name]

---

## Notes on customization

- Always personalize with a specific usage fact or milestone (shows you know the customer).
- The subject lines above perform well but A/B test against alternatives (see `guides/03-review-request-ux.md`).
- For enterprise accounts (>$50k ACV), replace the G2 link with a direct CSM request in a call before sending email.
- Never send Variant B until you've verified incentive compliance for the current quarter (`guides/02-g2-incentive-policy.md`).
