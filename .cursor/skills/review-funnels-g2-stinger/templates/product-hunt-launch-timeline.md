# Product Hunt Launch Timeline

Print this and use it as a day-of execution checklist. Check every box in sequence.

---

## 30 Days Before

- [ ] Research 5 successful launches in your category; note their taglines and first comments
- [ ] Identify 5 hunter candidates (1,000+ PH followers, recent track record)
- [ ] Reach out to top hunter candidate with a brief pitch
- [ ] Create Maker account at producthunt.com (if you don't have one)
- [ ] Begin building your "launch day list" (email subscribers, Slack/Discord community, LinkedIn connections who'd upvote)
- [ ] Confirm your launch date (Tuesday-Thursday preferred; avoid Mon/Fri)

## 14 Days Before

- [ ] Hunter confirmed; share all assets and launch date
- [ ] Thumbnail created: 240x240 PNG (icon or logo; no text unless it is your logo)
- [ ] Gallery images created: 1270x760 (5 images minimum; show product UI, not marketing copy)
- [ ] Product name finalized (≤25 chars)
- [ ] Tagline finalized (≤60 chars; benefit-oriented, no jargon)
- [ ] Product description written (plain English, ~100-200 words)
- [ ] Maker's first comment drafted (authentic, personal, origin story, 150-250 words)
- [ ] Launch day email drafted (sent at 12:05 AM PT)
- [ ] Community posts drafted (Slack, Discord, Twitter/X, LinkedIn)
- [ ] List of 5-10 personal contacts to text/DM on launch night

## 7 Days Before

- [ ] Hunter has built the draft listing; Maker reviews all copy and assets
- [ ] Optional video added to listing (under 60 seconds)
- [ ] Customer success team briefed: monitor PH comments for bugs, escalate immediately
- [ ] Team roles assigned: who monitors comments, who approves responses
- [ ] "Heads up" email sent to your list: "We're launching on Product Hunt on [date]"

## 3 Days Before

- [ ] Final review of all listing assets with hunter
- [ ] Launch day email finalized and queued in email platform (send time: 12:05 AM PT)
- [ ] Community posts queued (schedule manually -- do NOT auto-post from tool at odd hours)
- [ ] Pre-written responses to 5 anticipated comments or questions

## 1 Day Before

- [ ] Calendar alerts set: 11:50 PM PT, 12:01 AM PT, 12:05 AM PT
- [ ] Team on Zoom at 11:50 PM PT; everyone awake
- [ ] Confirm hunter is ready to submit at exactly 12:01 AM PT
- [ ] First comment pasted in a text file ready to post at 12:02 AM

## Post-Launch (Days 1-7)

- [ ] Export all PH comments; add respondents to CRM
- [ ] Follow up with users who left their email in comments
- [ ] Write "what we learned" blog post (draft within 48h; publish within 7 days)
- [ ] Add "Featured on Product Hunt" badge to homepage (see `guides/06-badge-deployment.md`)
- [ ] Send follow-up email to launch list with final ranking and thank-you
