# Product Hunt Launch Playbook: review-funnels-g2-stinger

## The single most important rule

**Launch at exactly 12:01 AM Pacific Time on your chosen date.**

Product Hunt resets its daily ranking at midnight Pacific. Any product posted after 12:01 AM PT starts the day with zero upvotes. A product launched at 8 AM PT has already lost 8 hours of ranking accumulation. There are no exceptions.

The first 6 hours drive ~65% of total day-of upvotes. The decision to win or lose #1 is often made by 6 AM PT.

Source: `research/external/2026-05-20-product-hunt-algorithm-first-hours.md`, `research/external/2026-05-20-product-hunt-launch-playbook.md`

## Pre-launch checklist (30/14/7/3/1 days)

### 30 days before

- [ ] Research successful launches in your category (study their gallery, taglines, comments)
- [ ] Identify 3-5 hunter candidates (1,000+ followers or existing track record of featured hunts)
- [ ] Reach out to top hunter candidate; explain the product and ask if they'd hunt it
- [ ] Create your Maker account if you don't have one (separate from user account)
- [ ] Begin building your "launch day list" (email subscribers, Slack community, LinkedIn connections)

### 14 days before

- [ ] Confirm hunter; share all assets and the launch date
- [ ] Prepare gallery assets:
  - Thumbnail: 240x240 PNG (icon, no text unless it IS your logo)
  - Gallery images: 1270x760 JPG/PNG (5 images recommended; show product, not marketing copy)
  - Video: optional but increases engagement; under 60 seconds
- [ ] Write product name (≤25 chars), tagline (≤60 chars), description (plain English, no jargon)
- [ ] Draft the Maker's "first comment" (authentic personal story; post it immediately after launch)
- [ ] Draft the "launch day ask" email (goes to your list at 12:01 AM PT)
- [ ] Identify 5-10 personal contacts to text/DM on the day for early upvotes

### 7 days before

- [ ] Build the PH listing in draft with your hunter (they submit; you coordinate)
- [ ] Prepare your community assets (Slack message draft, LinkedIn post, Twitter/X post)
- [ ] Prep customer success to monitor the PH comments section and escalate bugs
- [ ] Brief your team: who is monitoring comments, who is the designated responder

### 3 days before

- [ ] Final review of all gallery assets and listing copy
- [ ] Send a "heads up" email to your list: "We're launching on [platform] on [date]"
- [ ] Confirm all team members are awake by 12:00 AM PT (or arrange for someone to post the first comment)

### 1 day before

- [ ] Set calendar alerts: 11:55 PM PT (final prep), 12:01 AM PT (check listing live), 12:05 AM PT (send launch email)
- [ ] Confirm hunter has the listing ready to submit at 12:01 AM PT
- [ ] Prepare a batch of responses to anticipated questions in the comments
- [ ] Clear your day-of calendar

## Day-of timeline (hour-by-hour)

| Time (PT) | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| 11:55 PM (night before) | Final check: hunter ready, email queued, team awake |
| 12:01 AM | Hunter submits the listing |
| 12:02 AM | Maker posts the first comment (authentic, personal, origin story) |
| 12:05 AM | Send the launch email to your subscriber list |
| 12:10 AM | Post to your communities (Slack, Discord, LinkedIn, Twitter/X) |
| 12:15 AM | Text/DM your personal contacts: "We launched! 5 seconds to upvote?" |
| 12:30 AM | Check ranking position; respond to first PH comments |
| 1:00 AM | Second wave: post in relevant Slack/Discord communities (with permission) |
| 2:00-6:00 AM | Monitor comments; respond to every comment within 30 minutes |
| 6:00 AM | Share progress update with your team |
| 9:00 AM (US East Coast awake) | Send personal outreach to East Coast contacts |
| 12:00 PM | Mid-day pulse: monitor position, keep comment cadence |
| 5:00 PM (US West Coast end of workday) | Final push to your network |
| 11:59 PM | Final ranking captured; screenshot for social proof |

## Hunter vs. Maker: what each does

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|----------------|
| **Hunter** | Submits the listing; must have PH credibility (followers, past hunts); their name appears on the listing |
| **Maker** | Owns the product; responds to all comments; posts the first comment; claims the badges post-launch |

**Hunter sourcing criteria:**
- 1,000+ PH followers (minimum)
- History of products they've hunted reaching top 5 of the day
- Active in the community (recent posts, comments)
- Ideally, a genuine user or fan of your product (authenticity shows)

Finding a hunter: search PH for users who have hunted products in your category; reach out via Twitter/X DM or LinkedIn.

---

## Post-launch follow-through (days 1-7)

- Export all comments and questions; add responders to your outreach list.
- Request a "Product of the Week" feature (PH staff picks): reply in the comments with a gracious note and your traction numbers.
- Write a "what we learned" post (LinkedIn, company blog) within 48 hours; tag PH in it.
- Add the "Featured on Product Hunt" badge to your homepage (see `guides/06-badge-deployment.md`).
- Send a follow-up email to your launch list with final ranking and a "thank you" 24-48h post-launch.
