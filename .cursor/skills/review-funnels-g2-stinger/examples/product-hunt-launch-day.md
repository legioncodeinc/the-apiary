# Product Hunt Launch Day: Hour-by-Hour Execution

This example shows the actual execution of a Product Hunt launch day for a developer tool with ~300 newsletter subscribers and a small but engaged Slack community.

**Context:** Developer productivity tool, 280 email subscribers, 120 Slack community members, confirmed hunter (2,400 PH followers, 3 prior Product of the Day wins).

---

## Pre-launch (48 hours before)

- Listing reviewed by hunter and Maker; all gallery assets final.
- Launch email written, tested in email client, queued to send at 12:05 AM PT.
- Community posts drafted (Slack, Twitter/X, LinkedIn).
- First comment written, saved as a draft ready to paste at 12:02 AM.
- 8 personal contacts briefed: "I'm launching tonight at midnight. Can I count on you for an upvote right at 12:01?"
- Entire team awake on Zoom at 11:50 PM.

---

## Launch night (12:00 AM - 6:00 AM PT)

| Time | Action | Result |
|------|--------|--------|
| 11:55 PM | Final team check on Zoom | All confirmed ready |
| 12:01 AM | Hunter submits listing | Live immediately |
| 12:02 AM | Maker posts first comment (origin story, authentic) | 7 upvotes on comment within 10 minutes |
| 12:05 AM | Launch email sent to 280 subscribers | Opens start in 2 minutes |
| 12:08 AM | Personal text to 8 contacts | 6 upvotes within 15 minutes |
| 12:10 AM | Slack community announcement | 18 community members upvote |
| 12:20 AM | Twitter/X post from founder | 11 clicks, 4 upvotes |
| 12:30 AM | First ranking check: #6 for the day | Team continues monitoring |
| 1:00 AM | Respond to first 5 PH comments | Comment-to-comment response rate high |
| 1:30 AM | Post in 2 developer Slack communities (with moderator permission) | 9 additional upvotes |
| 3:00 AM | Ranking: #3 for the day | Morale high; continue |
| 5:00 AM | Ranking: #2 for the day | East Coast awake; new wave beginning |
| 6:00 AM | Email to East Coast contacts | Second wave of upvotes begins |

---

## Business hours (9 AM - 5 PM PT)

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 9:00 AM | LinkedIn post from founder (personal, not company page) |
| 10:00 AM | Twitter/X thread with launch story and early traction |
| 12:00 PM | Mid-day check: still #2 |
| 2:00 PM | Respond to all comments (27 total); no comment unanswered |
| 4:00 PM | Final push to remaining contacts |
| 5:30 PM | Ranking final: #2 for the day ("Product of the Day" for #1 only) |

---

## Final outcome

- Ended the day at #2 (just missed Product of the Day).
- Earned "2nd Product of the Day" status -- still a strong social proof asset.
- 112 new email signups from PH traffic.
- 38 new trial signups.
- 4 PH comments from users reporting bugs (all responded within 30 minutes; 2 became customers after follow-up).

---

## Retrospective

**What worked:**
- Hunter's credibility drove the early surge.
- Personal texts to 8 contacts gave the first 6 upvotes in minute one.
- Responding to every comment -- even short replies -- kept the listing visible in the "trending" feed.

**What to improve:**
- Needed a warm audience in more developer communities; only 2 approved communities were available day-of.
- First comment could have been more specific about the problem solved (it was too much origin story).
