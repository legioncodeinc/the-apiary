# Happy Path: G2 Review Funnel (0 reviews to Leader badge)

This example walks end-to-end execution of a G2 review-collection motion for a B2B SaaS product targeting mid-market buyers.

**Context:** B2B SaaS, project management category, 85 active customers, NPS score ~52 (mostly promoters), no existing G2 presence.

---

## Week 1-2: Profile setup

1. Claimed G2 profile at sell.g2.com. Completed all fields per `guides/01-platform-selection.md` setup checklist.
2. Added 8 screenshots showing core workflows (not onboarding screens).
3. Selected primary category: "Project Management Software."
4. Competitor comparisons added for top 3 alternatives.

**Outcome:** Full profile ready. No reviews yet but listing is discoverable.

---

## Week 3: Identify first 10 reviewers

From CRM, exported all customers with:
- NPS score 9-10 (28 customers qualified)
- Active in the last 30 days (21 of 28)
- No prior G2 review (all 21)

Selected top 10 by recency of NPS response and relationship strength.

---

## Week 4: First review campaign (non-incentivized)

Sent personal email from founder to 10 selected customers (template from `templates/review-request-email.md`, Variant A: no incentive).

**Subject:** "Quick favor from [Founder name]?"

**Results:** 4 reviews in 7 days (40% response rate -- high because personal and relationship-strong).
- Average rating: 4.6
- Profile now shows 4 reviews, 4.6 stars.

---

## Week 6-8: In-product happiness-check trigger

Implemented two-step pattern per `guides/03-review-request-ux.md`:

1. In-product modal triggered after user completes their 3rd project (milestone trigger).
2. Happiness check: 1-5 stars.
3. 4-5 stars → "Thanks! Would you share on G2?" (deep link).
4. 1-2 stars → "Thanks for letting us know. What can we improve?" (Typeform feedback form).

**Results (over 4 weeks):**
- Modal shown: 47 users
- Happiness check completed: 31 (66%)
- 4-5 stars: 22 users (71% of completers)
- Routed to G2: 22
- G2 reviews submitted: 8 (36% conversion from platform click to review submit)
- Total G2 reviews now: 12, average 4.5 stars

---

## Week 10: Crossed 20 reviews, earned "Users Love Us" badge

At 20 reviews and 4.5 average, the "Users Love Us" badge unlocked automatically.

- Added badge to homepage hero section (above fold).
- Added to pricing page between the two plan tiers.
- Added to CSM email signatures.

Conversion lift observed: pricing page CTA click-through increased ~12% in the following month (self-reported; not a controlled experiment).

---

## Month 6: Decision point -- invest in paid G2 plan?

At 47 reviews, 4.6 stars, Leader badge eligibility unlocked (would require ~$2,999/yr paid plan).

**Decision framework applied (from `guides/06-badge-deployment.md`):**
- ICP: mid-market ($15k-$50k ACV) -- G2 Leader carries strong credibility here.
- Pipeline: 3 enterprise deals in-flight had G2 as a shortlisting tool in their procurement process.
- ROI estimated: if 1 of 3 enterprise deals closed due to badge credibility and average ACV is $25k, the badge pays for itself immediately.

**Decision:** Purchased G2 Marketing Solutions. Earned "Leader - Summer 2025" badge.

---

## Ongoing cadence

- Monthly: Review NPS scores; trigger review request email for new promoters identified.
- Quarterly: Check G2 badge eligibility report (awards in Feb, May, Aug, Nov).
- Annual: Re-evaluate Trustpilot investment for SMB segment expansion.
