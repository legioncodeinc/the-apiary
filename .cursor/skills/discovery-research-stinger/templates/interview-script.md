## Act 3 — Switch story / Current-state story (10-15 min)

**Goal:** Get the JTBD story — the specific event that drove a behavior change.

Script starter:
- "Think about the last time you [made a decision about X / switched tools / started using Y]. Walk me through what happened, starting from the moment you first thought about it."

**Follow-up probes:**
- "What was going on that made this feel urgent at that specific moment?"
- "What were you doing before?"
- "What made you consider [new approach] at all?"
