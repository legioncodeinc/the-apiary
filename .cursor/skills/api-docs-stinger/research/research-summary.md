## Open questions resolved

- **Q: Is Scalar the recommended default over Redoc for new projects in 2026?**
  **A: Yes.** Multiple independent 2026 sources (APIScout, PkgPulse, Apidog) converge on this. Scalar is the default for new projects; Redoc remains preferred for large complex APIs requiring deep three-panel layout customization or read-only rendering.

- **Q: What is Fern's pricing and when does it become cost-prohibitive vs openapi-generator-cli?**
  **A: $250/month per SDK language** (after free OSS tier). Becomes cost-prohibitive when generating SDKs in 2+ languages. Use openapi-generator-cli (free) or Speakeasy (free for 1 lang / 250 endpoints) for multi-language targets.

- **Q: Does Bump.sh support private repos at a reasonable price point?**
  **A: Yes, private repos are supported.** Pricing details require checking bump.sh/pricing directly — not published in the documentation pages retrieved. The free public tier is well-established.
