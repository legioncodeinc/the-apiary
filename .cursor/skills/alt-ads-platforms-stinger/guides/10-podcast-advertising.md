# 10 — Podcast Advertising

Podcast advertising spans two channels: **Spotify Ad Studio** (programmatic, self-serve, scalable) and **direct/programmatic podcast networks** (host-read deals, RSS-based insertion, higher CPMs but stronger engagement). Both are distinct from YouTube or display advertising in that they reach audiences during commutes, workouts, and focused listening — high-attention contexts.

## Spotify Ad Studio

**The self-serve entry point for podcast and music advertising.** Minimum budget: $250 (campaign minimum). Practical minimum for meaningful data: $1,000/month.

### Streaming Intelligence Targeting

Spotify's targeting uses behavioral listening data:
- **Podcast category targeting:** Target listeners of specific podcast categories (True Crime, Business, Technology, Health & Fitness).
- **Real-time context targeting:** Target based on listening context (commuting, working, working out, studying).
- **Demographic + interest:** Age, gender, music taste profiles.
- **Moment targeting:** Target listeners in specific contexts (morning commute, afternoon work session, evening relaxation).

### Ad formats in Spotify Ad Studio

**Audio Ads (15-30 seconds):** The core format. Automatically reads between songs or podcast episodes. 95%+ completion rate because users cannot skip ads without premium subscription.

**Video Ads (Sponsored Sessions):** Full-screen video shown when user has screen in view. User watches to earn 30 minutes of ad-free listening. 40-60% completion rate.

**Podcast Ads (Insertion):** Spotify injects produced ads into podcast episodes programmatically. Different from host-read deals — produced ads perform at 60-70% of host-read effectiveness but are far easier to scale.

**Overlay Ads (Homepage Takeover):** Desktop-only banners. Not recommended as primary format.

### Creative requirements for Spotify audio

- 15-30 seconds (30 seconds recommended for new brand awareness).
- Professional voice recording required — no AI-generated voices (Spotify's listener base detects and reacts negatively to synthetic voice).
- Include a clear verbal CTA with a memorable URL or vanity URL.
- Background music is optional but helps with brand recall.
- Spotify's free audio creation tool is available in Ad Studio (Voice Talent feature) for simple ads.

## Host-read podcast ads (direct deals and programmatic networks)

**Host-read ads** are scripted but delivered in the host's voice as part of the podcast episode. They produce 2-3x higher recall than produced ads because listeners have established trust with the host.

**Channels for host-read ads:**
- **Direct deals:** Contact shows directly (email or form on their website). Best for shows in your exact ICP niche. Prices vary widely ($20-$100+ CPM for mid-size shows).
- **Acast:** Open marketplace for host-read ad placements across 100K+ shows. Self-serve or managed.
- **Spotify podcast marketplace:** Some shows on Spotify accept host-read deals through Anchor/Spotify for Podcasters.
- **Advertise Cast / Podcorn:** Platforms for matching brands to podcast shows for host-read deals.
- **AdvertiseCast directory:** Searchable database of shows with CPM rates.

## Attribution for podcast ads

Podcast attribution is the hardest attribution challenge in paid acquisition. Standard tracking approaches:

1. **Promo codes:** Unique discount code per show ("Use code LEGION at checkout"). Directly trackable. Requires checkout flow support.
2. **Vanity URLs:** Unique landing page URL per show (e.g., `yourproduct.com/techpodcast`). Redirects to main landing page. Trackable via URL analytics.
3. **Survey-based attribution:** Post-purchase "how did you hear about us?" survey. Captures bottom-of-funnel attribution including podcast listeners who never clicked a link.
4. **Pixel-based (Spotify):** Spotify's conversion pixel tracks clicks from Spotify's own interface only. Does not capture full podcast attribution.

**Recommended minimum:** Vanity URL + promo code combination for host-read deals. Survey-based attribution for scale.

## ICP fit for podcast advertising

**Strong fit:**
- B2C consumer product with broad demographic appeal.
- B2B product where the target audience listens to relevant niche podcasts (DevOps, marketing, entrepreneurship, finance).
- Brand awareness campaigns where the primary goal is recall and familiarity, not direct-response CPL.

**Weak fit:**
- Highly niche technical B2B products with a narrow ICP (total addressable audience too small for podcast reach).
- Products requiring visual demonstration (audio describes; it cannot show).
- Campaigns requiring precise conversion attribution at CPL-level precision.

## Budget guidance

**Spotify Ad Studio:** $1,000-$3,000/month for meaningful data and reach in a target category.

**Host-read deals (direct or programmatic):** $2,000-$10,000+/month depending on show size and CPM. Start with 3-month commitments to a small number of shows (2-3) before scaling.
