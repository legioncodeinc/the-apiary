# 06 — X/Twitter Ads

Best-fit use case: Brand awareness and thought leadership for B2B companies with existing X audience engagement. NOT recommended as a primary direct-response conversion channel.

---

## Platform volatility caveat

X/Twitter has experienced significant advertiser trust disruptions since 2022 following platform ownership changes, content moderation policy shifts, and brand safety concerns that led to major advertiser pullbacks. As of 2026, the platform has a smaller but more engaged advertiser base.

**Recommendation:** X/Twitter Ads should be tested only with a small supplementary budget ($500-$1,000/month) and only by companies with existing organic X presence. Do not allocate primary paid acquisition budget to X Ads without current market research.

---

## Ad formats

**Promoted Posts (formerly Promoted Tweets):** Boost existing organic posts or create ad-only content. Best for engaging content that generates replies and discussion.

**Amplify Pre-roll:** Video ads that run before premium publisher video content on X. Effective for brand awareness with verified brand-safe contexts.

**Follower Campaigns:** Grow X account following. Niche use case — primarily for brands where a large X following has downstream business value.

**Dynamic Product Ads:** Retargeting existing website visitors with product catalog ads. Available for e-commerce.

---

## Targeting options

- **Keyword targeting:** Target users who recently Tweeted or engaged with specific keywords.
- **Follower lookalike targeting:** Target users similar to followers of specific accounts (including competitor accounts).
- **Interest targeting:** Broad interest categories.
- **Conversation targeting:** Target users engaged in specific topic conversations.
- **Custom audiences:** Upload email lists for custom audience matching.

---

## X Pixel

The X Pixel tracks website conversions. Standard setup: add the pixel base code to `<head>`, then add event-specific tags for conversion pages (purchase, signup, lead).

Server-side conversion API availability — verify current status at X Ads developer documentation.

---

## When to use X Ads (narrow criteria)

Use X Ads only when ALL of the following are true:
1. Your company already has organic X presence with >500 followers and regular engagement.
2. Your target audience is demonstrably active on X (check: do your competitor accounts have engaged X audiences?).
3. The campaign goal is brand awareness or thought leadership, NOT direct-response CPL.
4. You have $500+/month budget for a 60-day test with no expectation of positive direct ROI.

If any of the above criteria are false, do not recommend X Ads. Redirect to LinkedIn, Reddit, or Microsoft Bing depending on ICP.
