# Creative Specs by Platform and Format (2026)

Reference this before briefing a designer or creative producer. Verify specs at each platform's official creative guidelines before production — specs change frequently.

---

## LinkedIn Ads

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect ratio | File size | File type | Copy limits | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Single Image Ad | 1200×627 px (recommended) | 1.91:1 | Max 5 MB | JPG, PNG, GIF | Headline: 150 chars; Intro text: 150 chars (truncated at 70 on mobile) | |
| Lead Gen Form (LGF) | Same as Single Image | 1.91:1 | Max 5 MB | JPG, PNG | Headline: 60 chars; Offer headline: 40 chars; CTA: 18 chars | Pre-fills from LinkedIn profile |
| Carousel Ad | Min 2, max 10 cards; 1080×1080 px each | 1:1 | Max 10 MB/image | JPG, PNG | Card headline: 45 chars | Last card can be a CTA card |
| Video Ad | 1920×1080 (16:9) or 1080×1080 (1:1) | 16:9 or 1:1 | Max 200 MB | MP4, MOV, AVI | Headline: 70 chars; Intro text: 150 chars | Captions recommended (85% watch without sound) |
| Document Ad | Max 10 pages | N/A | Max 100 MB | PDF | Headline: 70 chars | PDF pages become a swipeable preview |
| Thought Leader Ad | Uses existing LinkedIn post | N/A | N/A | N/A | Post copy: up to 1,300 chars | Can only boost a person's post (not company page post) |

## TikTok Ads

| Static image (carousel) | 1080×1080 or 1080×1920 | 1:1 or 9:16 | N/A | Max 500 KB per image | JPG, PNG | 2-35 images per carousel |
