## What success is NOT at any stage

- A single viral post (outcome, not strategy)
- Hitting a specific follower count milestone on a deadline
- Outperforming macro-influencer benchmarks (nano tier is the relevant comparison)
- Measuring success weekly (monthly or quarterly trends are signal; weekly is noise)
