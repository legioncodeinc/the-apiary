# social-media-marketing-organic-stinger

Equips `social-media-marketing-organic-worker-bee` with a practitioner-grade playbook for authentic, founder-led organic social growth on LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Threads, and Bluesky.

This stinger is built for solo developers and small product teams (up to ~10 people) who want genuine growth without AI-generated content factories, cross-post automation, or bought followers. The research baseline is 2026 benchmark data from SociaVault Labs (350K+ accounts), Foundera's LinkedIn 360Brew algorithm analysis, and practitioner guides from the build-in-public community.
