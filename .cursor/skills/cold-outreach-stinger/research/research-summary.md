## Key findings for stinger-forge by guide area

### `guides/01-tool-decision-matrix.md`
- Recommended founder stacks in 2026: (a) Apollo + Instantly for email-only volume, (b) Apollo + Smartlead for agency or multi-channel, (c) Apollo + Lemlist for high-personalization craft, (d) Smartlead alone for agency with LinkedIn + SMS
- Apollo does NOT run email warmup - always pair with a sending platform that does
- Lemlist's database has grown to 450M+ contacts and now includes waterfall enrichment - it may reduce Apollo dependency for some founders
- Instantly's largest advantage over Smartlead: flat pricing with unlimited accounts (no per-inbox math), A/Z testing depth, pipeline revenue attribution

### `guides/02-infrastructure-and-deliverability.md`
- Google permanent rejections (550 errors) live since November 2025 - this is not a warning, it's the current state
- Google Postmaster Tools v2 (October 2025): binary Pass/Fail replaces gradient reputation scoring
- Required 2026 configuration: SPF + DKIM (2048-bit) + DMARC (minimum p=none, recommended p=quarantine)
- Safe sending: 50-100 emails/mailbox/day; scale horizontally not vertically
- Warmup timing: 4 weeks minimum; key metric is reply rate during warmup, not sends

### `guides/03-sequence-design.md`
- Average reply rate benchmark: 3.43% (elite: 10%+, floor: >2% = healthy, <1% = broken)
- Optimal email length: under 80 words
- Timing: Wednesday peak; Tuesday-Thursday window; 7-11 AM local time for highest engagement
- Sequence length: 3-5 steps for most founders targeting SMB; 3-step actually generates highest per-sequence reply rate (9.2%) per Belkins data
- Multi-channel rule: 50% or less of total touchpoints should be email
- Step 2 tactic: write it to feel like a reply, not a reminder

### `guides/04-clay-personalization.md`
- Recommended waterfall chain: Prospeo → Hunter → Apollo (3 providers + MX validation = 70-85% coverage)
- Claygent throughput: 500+ contacts/hour at $0.02-0.05 per personalized line
- SKIP rule: if no specific data, Claygent returns "SKIP" - better than generic AI line
- Signal triggers for campaigns: job change (VP role = 3x higher buy probability in 90 days), funding event, tech stack change, job postings
- Clay's platform has 300,000+ users and 150+ data sources as of 2026

### `guides/05-list-hygiene.md`
- ZeroBounce: best accuracy (1.8% actual bounce after verify), $0.80/1K
- NeverBounce: best balance accuracy + price, $1.00/1K
- Catch-all domains: remove entirely for cold email (too risky)
- List decay: 25% per year - re-verify every 6 months minimum
- Gmail/Yahoo spam-filter domains with consistent >2% bounce rates
