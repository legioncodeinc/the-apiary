# Cold Email Sequence Template (5-Step)

Use this scaffold to build a cold email sequence. For SMB targets, use steps 1-3 only. For mid-market, use all 5 steps.

Reference: `guides/03-sequence-design.md`

## Step 1: Initial outreach (Day 0)

**Subject:** [3-7 words, no exclamation, prospect-specific]

Examples:
- `{{FirstName}} - {{Company}} + [your company]`
- `Question about [specific signal or topic]`
- `[Company]'s [specific challenge]`

**Body (target: 50-80 words):**

```
[Personalized opener — specific to this prospect, passes 1-in-1000 test, or skip to problem if no genuine hook]

Most [job title] I talk to are [specific problem]. [One-sentence bridge to your solution without feature-listing].

Would [15 minutes on Thursday or Friday] be worth it?

[FirstName]
```

**CTA:** Single, specific ask. Time slot or "worth a conversation?"

---

## Step 2: Follow-up #1 (Day 2-3)

**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

**Body (target: 30-50 words):**

```
[One new thought, resource, or angle — NOT "just following up"]

[Reference something new: a relevant stat, a case study in their industry, a different framing of the problem]

Still relevant?

[FirstName]
```

Rule: This should read like a reply in a thread, not a reminder.

---

## Step 3: Follow-up #2 (Day 7-10)

**Subject:** [New subject or Re: original]

**Body (target: 50-80 words):**

```
[Brief: acknowledge this is the third touch]

[Offer a different angle: specific use case, customer name-drop with permission, or a relevant piece of data]

[CTA: slightly softer — "Happy to send over more context if useful first?"]

[FirstName]
```

For SMB: this is the final step. End with breakup framing.

---

## Step 4: LinkedIn touch (Day 12-15) [Optional]

**Channel:** LinkedIn connection request or InMail

**Message (300 character limit for connection note):**

```
Hi {{FirstName}} — reached out by email about [one-sentence value prop]. Wanted to connect here too. No pressure.
```

Skip this step if you cannot commit to actually engaging on LinkedIn after connecting.

---

## Step 5: Breakup email (Day 18-21)

**Subject:** Closing the loop

**Body (target: 30-50 words):**

```
Hi {{FirstName}},

I won't keep reaching out after this one.

If [specific business outcome you help with] is ever on your radar, I'm easy to find. If not, no worries at all — happy to close your file.

[FirstName]
```

The breakup email often generates the highest reply rate in the sequence. Keep it genuine, not manipulative.
