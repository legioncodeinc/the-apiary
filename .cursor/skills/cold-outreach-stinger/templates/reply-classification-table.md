# Reply Classification Table

Use this table to classify every inbound reply and determine the correct next action. Copy this table into your CRM or tracking sheet.

Reference: `guides/06-reply-handling.md`

---

## Classification table

| Reply category | Signal phrases | Next action | SLA | Do NOT do |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Interested** | "Yes", "Happy to chat", "Send a time", "Tell me more", "Sounds relevant" | Book meeting within 4 hours | 4h | Do not over-explain in the reply; save pitch for the call |
| **Not now** | "Not right time", "Check back in Q3", "Mid-migration", "On the roadmap for next year" | Ask for specific follow-up date; mark CRM | 24h | Do not put back in cold sequence |
| **Wrong person** | "Talk to [Name]", "Not my decision", "Sits with IT / Finance / [team]" | Ask for warm intro or permission to contact directly | 4h | Do not cold email the redirected contact without permission |
| **Unsubscribe** | "Remove me", "Unsubscribe", "Stop emailing me" | Remove from all sequences; add to suppression; confirm removal | 1h | Do not argue or ask why; do not delay removal |
| **Angry** | "This is spam", "How did you get my email?", legal threats, spam reports | Remove immediately; send one-sentence apology; add domain to suppression | Immediate | Do not defend; do not ask for feedback; do not engage further |

## Tracking columns for CRM

Add these columns to your contact records:

| Column | Values |
|---|---|
| Reply category | interested / not-now / wrong-person / unsubscribe / angry / no-reply |
| Reply date | [YYYY-MM-DD] |
| Follow-up date | [YYYY-MM-DD] (required for not-now) |
| Disqualified | Y / N |
| Disqualification reason | too-small / too-large / wrong-title / no-budget / competitor / existing-customer |
| Notes | Free text |

## ICP disqualification criteria

Apply during response triage for "interested" and "not-now" replies:

| Criterion | Disqualify if... |
|---|---|
| Company size | Outside your ICP range (check against ICP worksheet) |
| Job title | Does not have budget authority for your product |
| Buying trigger | No active pain point or trigger detected |
| Company type | Competitor |
| Geography | Market you cannot serve |

For borderline cases, run one qualification email before booking the call:
> "Before we set time, quick question: [one specific qualifier question]?"
