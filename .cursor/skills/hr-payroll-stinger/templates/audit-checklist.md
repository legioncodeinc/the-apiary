## Section 1: Worker classification

- [ ] All US workers have been assessed against the IRS 3-category test
- [ ] Any California workers have been assessed against AB5 (ABC test)
- [ ] No long-term (12+ month) workers are classified as 1099 contractors for core business functions
- [ ] International workers are either on EOR, local entity payroll, or have been assessed for misclassification risk
- [ ] Any borderline classification cases have been reviewed by employment counsel

## Section 2: Payroll platform and registrations

- [ ] Company has an EIN (Federal)
- [ ] Company is registered with each state where W-2 employees work (income tax withholding)
- [ ] Company is registered for SUI (state unemployment insurance) in each employee state
- [ ] Workers' compensation coverage is in place in all employee states
- [ ] State new-hire reports have been filed within 20 days for all employees (all 50 states require this)
- [ ] Payroll platform is correctly configured for each state's withholding rates

**States with employees:**
| State | Income tax registered | SUI registered | Workers' comp | New-hire reports current |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| | | | | |

---

## Section 3: PFML obligations

- [ ] Company has identified which states have PFML mandates (CA, NY, NJ, WA, MA, CO, OR, CT, MN in 2026)
- [ ] PFML deductions are correctly configured in payroll for applicable states
- [ ] PFML employer contributions are being remitted (where required)

---

## Section 4: FLSA compliance

- [ ] All employees have been classified as exempt or non-exempt under FLSA
- [ ] All exempt employees are paid above the current salary threshold ($35,568/year as of 2026 — verify at dol.gov)
- [ ] Non-exempt employees are receiving overtime pay for hours over 40/week
- [ ] Computer professional exemption is being applied correctly (if applicable)

---

## Section 5: I-9 compliance

- [ ] I-9 forms are on file for all current employees
- [ ] I-9 forms were completed within 3 business days of each hire date
- [ ] Reverification has been completed for any employees with temporary work authorization
- [ ] I-9 forms are being retained for the required period (3 years from hire or 1 year after termination, whichever is later)

---

## Section 6: International compliance

- [ ] EOR agreements are in place for all international employees in countries where no local entity exists
- [ ] Local entity payroll is compliant in countries where the company has 5+ workers
- [ ] EU Platform Work Directive assessment has been conducted for any EU contractors (deadline: December 2, 2026)
- [ ] UK IR35 Status Determination Statements have been issued for UK contractors
- [ ] German workers have been reviewed post-2025 misclassification penalty law (€50,000/worker)

---

## Section 7: Equity and Carta

- [ ] 409A valuation is current (issued within 12 months or since last material event)
- [ ] Carta cap table is up to date (all current employees and option grants reflected)
- [ ] Payroll platform is connected to Carta for exercise event withholding
- [ ] 83(b) elections were filed within 30 days for any restricted stock grants
