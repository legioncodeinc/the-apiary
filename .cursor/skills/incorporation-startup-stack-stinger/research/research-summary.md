## Key findings for stinger-forge (executive synthesis)

### Entity type
- Delaware C-Corp = universal default for VC-backed software startups
- 89% of US venture-backed companies use this structure (Carta 2024)
- QSBS exclusion: up to $10M+ federal capital gains tax exclusion for stock held 5+ years
- LLC-to-C-Corp conversion is expensive ($8K+) and creates phantom tax liability on appreciated stock
- Delaware annual cost: $450/year ($400 franchise tax + $50 annual report fee), NOT the $76K Authorized Shares Method trap

- Mercury: $5M FDIC pass-through (not $3M as in Command Brief) - best for early-stage/international
- Brex Vault: $6M FDIC pass-through - best for VC-backed with large headcount

- Savings: $46,000+ per Pulley analysis of typical founder grants
