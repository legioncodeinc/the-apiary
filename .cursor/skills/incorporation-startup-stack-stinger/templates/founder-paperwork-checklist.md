## Phase 1: Entity formation

  - Bank: ___________________________

- [ ] **Bookkeeping platform connected** to bank feed
  - Platform: _______________________

---

## Phase 5: Optional but recommended

- [ ] **Cap table management tool set up** (Carta / Pulley — not included in formation platforms)
- [ ] **Business credit card applied for** (Brex / Ramp / Mercury card)
- [ ] **D&O / E&O insurance** (typically needed at Series A; optional at formation)
- [ ] **State tax registration** in states where you have employees or operations

---

## Attorney triggers (check if any apply — STOP and get counsel)

- [ ] International holding structure (Cayman holdco, etc.)
- [ ] Complex prior IP (from previous employer or university)
- [ ] Co-founder equity dispute unresolved
- [ ] SAFE or convertible note at formation
- [ ] Non-standard vesting terms
- [ ] Regulated industry (healthcare, fintech, defense)
