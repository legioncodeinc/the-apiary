The Apiary

Shared memory, and the hive around it, for your AI coding agents.
The problem

AI coding assistants are brilliant in the moment and forgetful the next. They forget what you told them yesterday, they open the wrong files, the local daemons that hold their memory die unnoticed, the dashboard is a port hunt, and once the stack spreads across machines nobody can see the fleet. The memory your agents need is fragile, scattered, and invisible.

The solution: one hive, five products

The Apiary is five small, sharp programs that fix it together, on hardware you control. One captures and serves memory, one makes your code searchable by meaning, and three keep the whole thing alive, visible, and yours.
