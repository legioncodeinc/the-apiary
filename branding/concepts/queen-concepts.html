Queen: logo concepts

The cloud fleet orchestrator. Round 3 leads with Mario's two mods: the Sovereign (filled crown over a split, hollow cell that reads like a face) and the inverted Signet (filled comb, crown as negative space). Rounds 1 and 2 survivors below. Same family DNA throughout: flat-top hexagons, 5px round strokes, one gradient element per mark.
