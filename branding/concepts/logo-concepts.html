Logo concepts

Every mark below is cut from the same cloth as the locked Honeycomb logo: flat-top hexagons, 5px round-joined strokes, and exactly one gradient-filled focal element per mark. Honey amber stays the family thread; each product owns one hue so you can tell the daemons apart in a dashboard, a favicon strip, or a terminal at 3am.
